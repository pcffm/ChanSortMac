# Privacy

ChanSort Mac 0.4.0 processes channel-list files locally on the user's Mac.

The application itself:

- does not require an account;
- does not contain advertising or tracking SDKs;
- does not collect analytics or application telemetry;
- does not upload channel lists, filenames or device data;
- does not sell or share personal data.

Links in the About & Legal window open the selected website in the user's default browser. The privacy policies of those external websites then apply. Operating-system services, download hosts and code-signing/notarization services are outside the application and may process standard connection metadata under their own terms.

Sender-list exports can contain network addresses, provider data, custom names or other user-entered information. Users control these files and should remove private material before attaching them to public issue reports.
